   **Static section** (hand-written guide content):
   - `= Deck Building Guide =` heading
   - Brief intro paragraph explaining Grid Tactics deck building
   - `== Basic Principles ==` — Mana curve advice (mix of cheap and expensive cards), element synergy (cards of same element may combo), tribe synergy (some tribes have mutual buffs)

   - `== Key Mechanics ==` — Brief pointers to [[Mana]], [[React Window]], [[Win Conditions]], [[Turn Structure]]

   - `== Current Archetypes ==` with a marker comment `<!-- AUTO-ARCHETYPES-START -->` and `<!-- AUTO-ARCHETYPES-END -->`

   - Add `[[Category:Rules]]` at the bottom

- Load card JSONs from `cards_dir` to count cards per element and per tribe for the archetype descriptions.
- Use brief, flavorful one-line descriptions per element archetype (derive from element names — Fire = aggressive, Water = control, Earth = defensive, etc.)
