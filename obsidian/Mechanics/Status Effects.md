# Status Effects

Persistent modifiers attached to a minion.

## Types
- **[[Burn|Burning]]** — boolean, ticks 5 damage on owner's turn start.
- **Buff / Debuff** — flat ATK/HP modifications. Floating popups display deltas (Phase 14.3).
- **Dark Matter** — buff stacks scale damage / scaling. Used by [[Cards/Grave Caller]].
- **Heal** — instant HP restore.

## UI
- Floating popups + persistent badges shipped in [[Phases/v1.1/Phase 14.3 Game Juice]].
