# Transform

Pay mana to morph a minion into another form. Includes **Promote** (on-death transform).

## Active Transform
- Costs mana from the player's [[Mana System|pool]].
- Replaces the minion in place, preserving position.
- [[Cards/Reanimated Bones]] — can transform into [[Cards/Pyre Archer]] (2), [[Cards/Grave Caller]] (3), or [[Cards/Fallen Paladin]] (4).

## Promote (On-Death)
- [[Cards/Giant Rat]] — promotes from a [[Cards/Common Rat]] on death (the rat transforms into Giant Rat).
