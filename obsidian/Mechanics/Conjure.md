# Conjure

Summon a card from **outside your deck** directly to the board. Distinct from [[Tutor]] (which searches the deck).

## Users
- [[Cards/Ratchanter]] — Activated ability conjures a [[Cards/Common Rat]] token onto an empty own-side tile

## Rules
- Token minions do not return to the deck.
- Subject to summoning rules (empty tile, board not full).
