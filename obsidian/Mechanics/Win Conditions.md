# Win Conditions

Two paths to victory.

## 1. 🤍 Depletion
- Reduce opponent 🤍 to 0 via damage (combat, magic, [[Burn]], [[Sacrifice]]).

## 2. Sacrifice
- Cross the board with a minion and [[Sacrifice]] for damage.

## Draw
- Mutual death or simultaneous 0 🤍 — handled by `game_state.py`.
