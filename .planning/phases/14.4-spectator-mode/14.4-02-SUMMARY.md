# Phase 14.4 Plan 02: Spectator View Filter Summary

**One-liner:** Pure-function spectator filter — god mode deep-copies full state, non-god delegates to filter_state_for_player(perspective_idx).

## What Was Built

Added `filter_state_for_spectator(state_dict, god_mode, perspective_idx=0)` to `src/grid_tactics/server/view_filter.py`.

Two modes:

- **god_mode=True** — `copy.deepcopy(state_dict)`. Both hands, both decks (with card order), pending tutor matches, and seed all remain visible.
- **god_mode=False** — delegates to `filter_state_for_player(state_dict, perspective_idx)`. Spectator sees exactly what player `perspective_idx` sees. Inherits opponent hand stripping, deck hiding, seed removal, and (when later combined with `enrich_pending_tutor_for_viewer`) pending-tutor opponent stripping.

Always stamps three top-level flags so the client can branch:
- `is_spectator: True`
- `spectator_god_mode: bool`
- `spectator_perspective: int`
